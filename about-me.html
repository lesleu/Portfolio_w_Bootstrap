Alittle bit about me...

Leselle Leuterio is a multi-faceted digital artist & designer with a passion for creating immersive visuals. She works in 2D, 3D, and in motion.
As a avid music and arts lover, she has created a live visual business where she creates custom content for DJs - as well as performs live with them as a VJ. In her free time she enjoys learning and exploring new technology such as projection mapping, augmented reality, digital fashion, and NFTs. For those inbetween moments - shes a big family person, sci-fi fan, skateboarder, and snowboarder.

My Experiences

Graduate from Full Sail University in Florida, I studied graphic design. After graduated, I freelanced work with clients from various industries.
