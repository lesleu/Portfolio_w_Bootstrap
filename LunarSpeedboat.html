Project Overview

Lunar Speedboat Productions had a mission to create this otherworldly logo animation - so they came to me.

Their vision was to use their logo mark as a boat traveling through metallic water. With such an ambitious task, I brought along a close colleague of mine was proficient in simulations using Houdini.

The Process

Working remotely was no difficult feat with a strong and dedicated team.

Togs and tons of iterations took place in creating this otherworldly and semi-realistic animation. It was important for me and my partner to communicate clearly and be highly organized.

Programs used:

Trello - Project Management

Cinema 4D - Modeling, Texturing, Lighting, Animation

Houdini - Water Simulation
