Project Overview

Asteria Arts & Music Festival is a grassroots local festival in Florida that has been constantly creating lineups and experiences that compete with the big leagues. My mission as their content creator was to create a digital experience that grew excitement leading up to the event. This was done by creating cohesive designs and engaging video content that made Asteria’s branding look professional and evoke a story.
